About

*Image of us here?*
MadLads is a fun fill-in-the-blank story game website. The site was created by some Mad Lads at the University of Montana. Jan Roddy, Catey Meador, Sean Rice, and Kody Neill created MadLads for their Software Science class in the Fall of 2019. MadLads is derivative of the game MadLibs, with (hopefully) some additional cool features.
