About

MadLads is a fun fill-in-the-blank story game website.
The site was created by some Mad Lads and Ladies at the University of Montana.
Jan Roddy, Catey Meador, Sean Rice, and Kody Niell created MadLads for their Software Science class in the Fall of 2019.
MadLads is derivative of the game MadLibs, with some cool new features.
We hope you enjoy our game!
